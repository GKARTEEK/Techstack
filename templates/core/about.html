About TechStack

Your ultimate resource for technical interview preparation.

Our Mission

TechStack was founded with a simple mission: to help developers prepare for technical interviews by providing a comprehensive repository of interview questions across multiple programming languages.

We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality interview preparation resources, regardless of their background or experience level. Our platform is designed to be accessible, user-friendly, and comprehensive.

Our Team

At TechStack, there is no separate team—it's just you and me working together to build the best collection of interview questions.

As the admin, you manage and curate the questions, while our customers contribute by sharing their interview experiences. Together, we create a valuable resource for anyone preparing for technical interviews.

How It Works

TechStack organizes interview questions by programming language, topic, and difficulty level. You can browse questions, search for specific topics, and bookmark questions for later review.

Our community-driven approach allows users to submit their own interview questions, which are reviewed by our team before being added to the repository. This ensures that our content remains relevant and up-to-date.

Join Our Community

TechStack is more than just a question repository—it's a community of developers helping each other succeed in technical interviews.

By creating an account, you can bookmark questions, track your progress, and contribute to the community by submitting your own interview questions.
